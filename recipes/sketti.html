Sketti

Description

Only settle for the best sketti, even if it makes you feel a little sick.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Ketchup
  • Pasta of some kind but we all know butter is the star of the show
  • Hot sauce - cause why the hell not?
  • Your bare hands - no need for a fork

Steps

  1. Cook the living bejesus out of the pasta. Until it falls apart with just a whisper (not a careless one)
  2. Melt the butter - preferrably with a microwave but you do you.
  3. Dump the ketchup in a pan and add all that delicious butter.
  4. Add hot sauce. Right meow.
  5. Dump everything into a bowl and mix it together with your hands like an animal.
  6. Nom nom nom. Feel free to vomit if needed. (Seriously how the hell did honey boo boo eat this crap?!)
